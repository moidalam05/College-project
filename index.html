  • Lotus Temple

    The Lotus Temple, located in Delhi, India, is an architectural marvel and a symbol of unity, peace, and spirituality. Shaped like a blooming lotus flower, this breathtaking Bahá'í House of Worship welcomes people of all religions and backgrounds to meditate, reflect, and find inner peace.

  • Bodh Gaya

    Bodh Gaya, a small but profoundly significant town in the Indian state of Bihar, holds a place of utmost reverence in the hearts of millions worldwide. It is here, beneath the Bodhi Tree, that Siddhartha Gautama, the historical Buddha, attained enlightenment more than 2,500 years ago.
